Rosemary Stuffed Pork Loin

Image used with permission through the Creative Commons Zero licence via Pexels.

Based off of the Rosemary Stuffed Pork Loin recipe by Laura Vitale at laurainthekitchen.com

Ingredients

  • 1 2-1/2 lb Pork Tenderloin
  • 6-8 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Springs of Fresh Rosemary
  • 1/2 Bunch of Italian Parsley
  • Olive Oil
  • 4 oz of Thinly Sliced Pancetta
  • 2 cups of White Wine
  • 1/2 cup of Water
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste
